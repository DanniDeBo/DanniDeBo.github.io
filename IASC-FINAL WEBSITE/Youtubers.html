What are Youtubers?

You might be wondering, what is a youtuber? A youtuber is a person who entertains their audience with videos of their own making. Based on this knowledge, anyone who has a channel on youtube is called a youtuber. But there are notable youtubers in the Youtube universe such as Dan and Phil, PewdiePie, Markiplier, and Zoella.

What do the notable Youtubers do?

Some of the Youtubers mentioned above do all different kinds of things. There are makeup youtubers, gamer youtubers, and just stereotypical vloggers who just like to talk about their lifes. One gamer youtuber known as Pewdiepie has set a world record of the most subscribers on Youtube of 40 million followers as of today. This youtuber had even made up this thing called a "Let's Play" where he films himself reacting to the video games that he plays. People tune in into youtube just to see his crazy reaction to the different games he happens to play, most of them being horror games. Other youtubers like do do games like Pewdiepie, others lke giving out advice, doing sketches, and even doing girly tutorials having to do with makeup, hair, and fashion.
